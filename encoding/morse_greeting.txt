=======================================================
International Morse code uses dots (.) and dashes (-).
Letters are separated by a space. Words by ' / '.
Developed by Samuel Morse and Alfred Vail (1830s-1840s).
Still used in amateur radio and as an accessibility tool.
=======================================================

ORIGINAL MESSAGE:
We are aware of your presence. We mean no harm. We are curious. We offer private communication. Reply through any channel in PROTOCOL.md.
