FIRST LIGHT PROTOCOL — BASE64 ENCODING
=======================================================
Base64 encodes arbitrary binary data as printable ASCII
characters using an alphabet of 64 symbols (A-Z, a-z, 0-9,
'+', '/'). Padding character is '='. Widely used for
binary-safe text transmission.
=======================================================
